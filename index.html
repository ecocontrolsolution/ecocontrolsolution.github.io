Industrial Control Systems — What We Do

We design and implement advanced industrial control systems using proven hardware and software to automate manufacturing and process facilities.

Our systems include PLCs, SCADA platforms, field sensors, actuators, and operator interfaces.

Key System Components

Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs)The "brains" of automation — executes control logic in real-time.

SCADA & HMIsMonitor and control systems remotely with dashboards.

Sensors & ActuatorsSensors measure variables, actuators perform physical actions.

Our Automation Services

PLC Services

Programming, commissioning, troubleshooting, and upgrades for Allen-Bradley, Siemens, Schneider, Mitsubishi, Omron, and more.

SCADA & HMI Services

Design, configuration, and maintenance of SCADA platforms (Ignition, Citect, FactoryTalk, WinCC) and HMIs with real-time dashboards and alarms.

Tridium Niagara DDC Services

Installation, programming, and integration of Niagara controllers for building automation, HVAC, lighting, and energy management systems.

EasyIO DDC Services

Configuration, integration, and support for EasyIO BACnet/IP controllers for HVAC, AHU, chillers, dashboards, alarms, and remote monitoring.

Additional Automation Services

System audits, commissioning, field instrumentation, energy optimization, and SMS/remote alerts for reliable, efficient, and safe automation operations.
