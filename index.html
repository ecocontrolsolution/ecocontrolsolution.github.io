Industrial Control Systems — What We Do

We design and implement advanced industrial control systems using proven hardware and software to automate manufacturing and process facilities.

Our systems include PLCs, SCADA platforms, field sensors, actuators, and operator interfaces.

Key System Components

Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) The "brains" of automation — executes control logic in real-time.

SCADA & HMIs Monitor and control systems remotely with dashboards.

Sensors & Actuators Sensors measure variables, actuators perform physical actions.

Our Core Services

PLC Automation & Control: Supply, design, programming and troubleshooting for industrial PLCs — including Allen‑Bradley, Siemens, Schneider, Mitsubishi and Omron controllers.

SCADA Systems: Implementation and integration of Citect, Ignition, FactoryTalk, WinCC and custom HMI solutions for remote visualization and control.

Data Historian & Reporting: Configure and manage historian systems for long‑term data storage, trends and analytics to support compliance and performance insight.

Electrical Engineering Services: Tailored electrical design, installation and commissioning for control panels, switchboards, wiring and power systems.

SMS Alarm & Remote Access: Real‑time alerts and remote diagnostics to keep your systems safe and up‑to‑date 24/7.

Why Industrial Automation Matters

Automation increases production efficiency by reducing manual intervention and improving safety with real-time monitoring.
